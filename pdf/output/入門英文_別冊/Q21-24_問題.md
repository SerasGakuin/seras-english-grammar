### 21　→ 解答　本冊 68ページ

Even inventions that meet the need for which they were initially designed may later prove more valuable at meeting unforeseen needs.

### 22　→ 解答　本冊 70ページ

①When Edison built his first \*phonograph in 1877, he published an article proposing ten uses to which his invention might be put. ②They included preserving the last words of dying people, recording books for blind people to hear, and teaching spelling.

\* phonograph「蓄音機」

（熊本大学）

### 23　→ 解答　本冊 72ページ

①The combined data showed that those who consumed a moderate amount of coffee, about three to five cups a day, were at the lowest risk for problems. ②Those who consumed five or more cups a day had no higher risk than those who consumed none.

（愛知教育大学）

### 24　→ 解答　本冊 74ページ

One group was told that they were going to get feedback, some of which was good and some of which was bad, and were asked which they wanted to hear first.
