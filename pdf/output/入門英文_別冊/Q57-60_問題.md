### 58　→ 解答　本冊 142ページ

①... behaving in the same way that you would at home can land you in fairly serious trouble. ②In England, it is standard practice to take wine to a dinner party, but the same does not apply in France. ③To do so would suggest that your hosts are unable to choose or afford a good bottle.

（甲南大学）

### 60　→ 解答　本冊 146ページ

（神戸大学）
