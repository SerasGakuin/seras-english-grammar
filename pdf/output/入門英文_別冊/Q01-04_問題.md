### 1　→ 解答　本冊 28ページ

①With more and more disposable, packaged goods, the average household's volume of garbage \*skyrocketed.

\* skyrocket「急増する」

（津田塾大学）

### 2　→ 解答　本冊 30ページ

①At the 2012 Summer Olympics, for example, more than 10,000 competitors from 204 nations and regions participated. ②These games were attended by thousands of spectators and watched by millions of people on television.

（東海大学）

単語の意味はこのページをめくったところにあります。参考にしましょう。

### 3　→ 解答　本冊 32ページ

①Most of the friendships were reported as being supportive. ②Family, however, were found to have little influence on an individual's health and well-being.

（藤女子大学）

### 4　→ 解答　本冊 34ページ

①I think students should reduce their time in front of screens and should read books every day even for a short time. ②Forming a reading habit in childhood is said to be associated with later reading proficiency.

## 読むヒント

**1** 主語はどこにある？
**2** 第2文はなぜ受動態になっているの？
**3** were found の後ろの to have はどこにつながる？
**4** is said の後ろの to be associated はどこにつながる？
